We Build. Digital. Shapers.

We are TechLabs. An interdisciplinary team from various study programs - with and without tech backgrounds - but united in one mission: Teaching digital skills to young tech enthusiasts, thus fostering a new generation of digital thinkers, who will set the base for our digital future within their area of expertise.

TechLabs Tracks: Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is the technology of the future. Learn and understand how AI is built, how it works and which areas you can apply it to. We will teach you all that in our AI track.

TechLabs Tracks: Web Development

Web Development is a software engineering discipline that involves the development of websites and web services. Web Development can range from creating a basic static web page to complex interactive web apps, including enterprise-scale applications and application programming interfaces.

TechLabs Tracks: Data Science

Data Science is a rapidly growing discipline. The need for data scientists is strong - not only in the startup world. But what do data scientists actually do? Learn how to detect exciting trends from real data sets and predict events much more precisely.
